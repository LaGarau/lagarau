About Us

La Garau is a community-driven initiative born from a simple yet powerful idea: together, we can accomplish anything. Our name derives from the Nepali phrase "ल गरौं !" which translates to "Let's do it!"

We bring together innovators, creators, and visionaries from diverse backgrounds to collaborate on projects that create meaningful impact in our communities and beyond.

Our Plan

Ghumante Yuwa

Ghumante Yuwa is a planned initiative to create a gamified scavenger hunt that will engage youth in Kathmandu. The goal is to revitalize local tourism through a series of educational challenges and real-world exploration, encouraging young people to discover the city’s cultural gems. By gamifying the experience, we aim to drive foot traffic to local businesses and promote sustainable tourism. This plan will provide a platform for youth to connect with their community, learn about their heritage, and contribute to the local economy in a fun and interactive way.

Our Philosophy

"The question isn't 'Can we do it?' but rather 'How will we do it together?'"

At La Garau, we believe in the power of collective action. We embrace challenges as opportunities for growth and innovation. Our approach combines ambitious vision with practical execution, always centered on creating lasting positive impact.

We're guided by these principles:

  • Inclusivity - Everyone has something valuable to contribute
  • Sustainability - Creating solutions that endure
  • Transparency - Open collaboration and honest communication
  • Adaptability - Evolving with changing needs and circumstances
